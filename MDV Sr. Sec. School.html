Infrastructure



Smart Class


A lesson well caught is a lesson well taught". With the sincere mission to transport MDVeens to the opulent shore of learning, to endow the MDVeens with the zest and zeal for knowledge to make teaching-learning experiences enjoyable and meaningful, MDVSSS caters to the students the best of the amenities. Educomp moduls and Smart Boards have been introduced in the classes to make the classes tech savvy, to ensure better understanding of the topics through the use of visual aids and to make learning more realistic and interesting.



Swimming Pool


MDVSSS boosts of an exquisite pool with dimension of an Olympic size, with efficient water filters changing rooms for boys and girls, trained swimming coach with life guard, Oxygen cylinders, Medical aid / facilities, completely sanitized pool with latest equipment, safe proportions of chlorine and alum content are ensured for optimal fitness and to avoid unnecessary chemical intake.
To insure further safety, small children are duly escorted to the pool by class teachers and supporting staff.
